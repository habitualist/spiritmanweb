Say the Salvation Prayer

“O Lord God, I come to You today in the Name of Jesus.
I believe in my heart that Jesus Christ is the Son of God.
I believe He died for me and that God raised Him from the dead.
I confess with my mouth that Jesus Christ is the Lord of my life from this day.
Thank You, Lord, for saving my soul.”
